## Overview At Check24, I worked on optimizing the user experience for high-traffic comparison platforms, focusing on mobile flows and implementing chatbot features to improve engagement.

## The Challenge Users were dropping off during mobile comparison flows. The interface needed to be faster, clearer, and more conversational to match user expectations on mobile devices. **Key Problems:** - High bounce rates on mobile comparison flows - Complex decision-making process overwhelming mobile users - Lack of conversational guidance in user journey - Inconsistent mobile experience across different comparison categories
## My Approach ### Product Discovery Analyzed user behavior data and conducted mobile usability tests to identify friction points in the comparison journey. ### Feature Implementation Built React-based chatbot interface for guided comparisons, simplifying complex decision-making processes through conversational UI. ### Optimization A/B tested different mobile layouts and interaction patterns to validate improvements before full rollout. ### QA & Iteration Rigorous testing across devices and browsers, followed by rapid iteration based on data feedback.
### Outcomes - **25% improvement** in mobile conversion rate - **Reduced user drop-off** in comparison flows significantly - Successfully **translated business requirements** into technical implementation - Gained experience with **high-traffic platforms** (millions of monthly users) - Developed **data-driven product mindset** using analytics and A/B testing
## Key Learnings Consumer products taught me: - **Speed and conversion as primary metrics**: Every millisecond and click matters at scale - **Importance of rapid iteration and testing**: Ship, measure, learn, repeat - **Balancing user needs with business goals**: Finding win-win solutions - **Mobile-first thinking**: Touch targets, thumb zones, simplified interactions - **Data-driven decision making**: Let user behavior guide product decisions
